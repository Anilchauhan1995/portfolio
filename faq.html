Can I modify my order?

An order can be cancelled before the preparation is started. Once that is done, a new order can be placed again as per the modifications. If the order is in process of being prepared, we can't assure that we'll be able to change your order.

Why was my order cancelled?

An order might be canceled for one of these reasons: 1. The outlet ran out of one (or more) items you ordered 2. The outlet couldn't process your order due to technical reasons beyond our control 3. Our Delivery person couldn't get in touch with you or find the address mentioned 4. The coupon code was not applied 5. The order is already placed & this is a duplicate order 6. Our outlets are currently not serving in your locality A cancellation message is sent once our Delivery person updates us that they can't reach you after multiple attempts. We request you to keep your phone nearby so that you can be reached if needed. In case of cancellation based on points 1 & 2, the order amount will be refunded in 4-6 days..

8PASS Query

What is 8PASS?
8PASS is a special supersaver membership program that gives you FLAT 25% OFF upto Rs. 300 on each order. There is no minimum order value required (other than the minimum order value of an outlet which is Rs.99). There are 2 kinds of 8PASS - 8PASS TRIAL & 8PASS PRO. In the TRIAL version, you get 25% OFF on 5 orders & validity of 90 days, while in the PRO version you get 25% OFF on all orders & validity of 180 days.

Account Settings

I forgot my password. What should I do?

If you forgot your password, you can alternatively login via OTP using your mobile number, without any hassle. To change your password follow these steps: 1. Tap on Forgot Password? button on your log-in screen. 2. Enter your registered Email ID or Phone Number 3. You'll receive an email/SMS with a unique verification code. 4. Use the verification code to create a new password. Do not share your password with others. Remember, BOX8 support will never ask for your password.
